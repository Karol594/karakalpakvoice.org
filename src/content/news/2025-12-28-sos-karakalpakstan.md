Human rights defenders Elena Urlaeva (Uzbekistan) and Bagdatbay Ismailov (Karakalpakstan) conducted joint human rights monitoring from December 14 to 20, 2025, reviewing the state of political, economic, and social rights in the Republic of Karakalpakstan.

Bagdatbay Ismailov informed Elena Urlaeva that “During the USSR era, the Karakalpak Autonomous Soviet Socialist Republic was an economically developed region; the Karakalpaks were engaged in animal husbandry, and there were many poultry farms and livestock farms in every district and every state farm.
